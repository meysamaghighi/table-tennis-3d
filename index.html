Equipment Types

Inverted Rubber

Maximum spin potential. The ball grips the surface for heavy topspin and serves. Best for attackers.

Anti-Spin

Reverses incoming spin. The opponent's topspin becomes backspin. Excellent for blocking and control.

Long Pimples

Highly disruptive. Opponent's spin is returned unpredictably. Low speed, tricky to play against.

Short Pimples

Flat, fast hits with low spin. The ball skids off the rubber. Great for aggressive close-to-table play.

Tips

  • Time your swing as the ball approaches for clean contact
  • Angle the paddle up for topspin, down for backspin
  • Different rubbers react completely differently to spin
  • Carbon blades are fast but harder to control
  • Watch the ball's rotation to read incoming spin
